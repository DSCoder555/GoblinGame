Goblin Game!




How to Play

You will play against both unK and glooB as a team. The goblinS will go first.
On their turn, the goblinS will take 1 stone from the pile with the most stones in it.
On your turn, click a button to indicate from which pile you want to remove stones from (and how many stones). You cannot take a stone from an empty pile.
Whoever takes the last stone loses and the game will end.
You can click the reset button at any time and all of the stone piles will reset to 3 stones and the game will start over.
